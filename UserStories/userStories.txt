This website is intended to offer services to individuals who may need help designing and developing
a new product. These new products may include circuit boards, enclosures, software, or testing of items.
The services offered are intended to supplement existing staff where there are gaps in resources or
technical expertise.


As a manager I want to find a contractor to design a circuit board.
As an electrical engineer I want to find someone to help write software
As a inventor I want to find someone to help bring my idea to reality.
As a user I want to contact the firm about my project.
