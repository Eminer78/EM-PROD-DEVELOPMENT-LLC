Our Expertise

EM Product Development, LLC specializes in Electrical, Electro-Mechanical, and Software Engineering. The firm offers services from ideation to product launch.

Our Firm offers services associated with each of the standard stages of a New Product Development project.

  • Product Definition -

    Define the product and its functional requirements and characteristics

  • Feasibility Studies -

    Proof-of-Concept generation for feasibility studies

  • Development -

    Fully develop the product to incorporates all the required features and characteristics

  • Validatation -

    Testing and analysis of the product

  • Launch -

    Refinement and transfer of the product into mass production
